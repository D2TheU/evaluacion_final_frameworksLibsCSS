Bacon Egg Bowls

25 de Julio 2017

In a pan over medium heat, fry the bacon until crispy. Transfer the bacon to a plate lined with paper towels to absorb excess oil. Set aside.

Whisk the eggs, milk, salt, freshly ground black pepper and nutmeg in a bowl. For extra flavor, you can add any herb or seasoning of your choice.

Slice the tops off the buns and remove the bread from the center, creating bowls. Place the bowls on a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil. Evenly distribute the bacon and cheese between the bread bowls, then pour the egg mixture on top.

Bake at 320 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes, until the egg has set and is lightly browned. Remove from the oven and enjoy!
